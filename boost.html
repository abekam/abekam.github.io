Enhancing the Capabilities of Recently Employed Sales Representatives

This non-training interventional project outlines efforts to enhance the competencies of recently employed sales representatives in ways other than regular training. The project pinpointed areas for development and proposed eight interventions to achieve organizational objectives for customer satisfaction .
