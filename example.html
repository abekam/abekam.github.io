Ernie

Helga is the latest addition to the GLM sales contact center. Like many of the new employees , she is overwhelmed with new information about the company's processes and products . Usually, if Helga has trouble on a call, she gets a bit nervous and puts the client on hold and reaches out to her supervisor.
Here comes Ernie! An interactive chatbot that acts as a first line helper for newly hired employees. Helga can now chat with Ernie and easily find answers to her questions instead of interrupting the work flow. Ernie's keyword-based module helps new employees build expertise and promotes independance by providing them with seamless access to searchable help and Q&A sections while they are dealing with clients.
